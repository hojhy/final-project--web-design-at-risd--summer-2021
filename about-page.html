Sunny Han is an interdisciplinary artist who primarily paints surrealism. She deconstructs and processes her perception through spiritual practices and transcripts it into her art. Exploring different forms of communication and mediums, Sunny works towards unbinding existing paradigms to figure out a the most effective way to convey her intentions.

Sunny is currently finishing her BFA in Painting at Maryland Institute College of Art and is currently a Arist in Residence at the Carrie Able Gallery in New York City, U.S.

The artist statements are important parts of her work so please read them!
